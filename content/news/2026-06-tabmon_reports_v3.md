## Updated reports for all TABMON clusters

We have just published **version 3** of the TABMON per-cluster reports on Zenodo. This update incorporates the latest data collected across our monitoring network and includes refined BirdNET analysis results for **all 30 clusters** spanning Norway, the Netherlands, France, and Spain.

## What's in the reports?

Each cluster report contains:

- **Device uptime** — How long each recorder has been operational and data completeness metrics
- **Species detections** — Full lists of bird species detected at each site with their conservation status
- **Vocalisation patterns** — Number of vocalisations detected per species, per device, and per cluster
- **Temporal dynamics** — Activity patterns across hours of the day and seasons

The reports cover sites from the Arctic (Pasvik, Varanger) to the Mediterranean (Aiguamolls de l'Empordà, Delta de l'Ebre, Porquerolles).
