<div class="pub-section-intro">
<p>TABMON's scientific output spans multiple disciplines including <strong>acoustic ecology</strong>, <strong>artificial intelligence</strong>, <strong>biodiversity monitoring</strong>, and <strong>conservation policy</strong>. Our research contributes to advancing both methodological frameworks and empirical understanding of European biodiversity patterns.</p>
